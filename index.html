The method

  1. Define the soul. Find the emotional centre, commercial aim and cultural code.
  2. Build the machine. Create the prompts, references, models and pipelines.
  3. Explore at scale. Generate breadth without confusing volume for quality.
  4. Curate by taste. Select, edit, refine and finish the work by hand.
